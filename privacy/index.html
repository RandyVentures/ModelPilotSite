Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026

ModelPilot is designed to minimize data collection. The app primarily displays curated catalog/update content and local settings.

Data We Collect

By default, ModelPilot does not require account login and does not collect personal profile data directly.

Local Data

Preference settings may be stored locally on your device (for example: theme and feed preferences).

Remote Content

The app may fetch model/update JSON from configured endpoints for content refresh. Those requests may be logged by the hosting provider.

Contact

For privacy questions, open a support request: GitHub Support.
